## Post 2 — Four-Part Cold Email Secret (Podcast Summary)

From his Sales Evangelist podcast appearance:
> "You're overcomplicating it. Reread your email, and if it needs a better flow, put a random first sentence, a random problem statement, and a random solution. If those don't talk together, it's a bad email."

His 4-part framework:
1. **Relevant hook** — something specific to the prospect's world
2. **Problem statement** — one thing they actually experience
3. **Solution bridge** — how your offer connects
4. **Soft CTA** — not "book a call," but "would this be useful?"

**Source:** Sales Evangelist Podcast, June 2024 (https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/vin-matano-my-four-part-cold-email-secret/id788738885)
**Key insight:** Internal consistency check — if the three parts (hook, problem, solution) don't flow naturally, the email won't convert regardless of copy quality.
